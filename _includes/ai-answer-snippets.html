What is korfball?

Korfball is a mixed-gender team sport that combines elements of netball and basketball. Played with 8 players (4 men, 4 women), teams score by shooting a ball through a basket (korf) 3.5 meters high. Originating in the Netherlands, korfball emphasizes teamwork, gender equality, and tactical play.

What are Newport Centurions' achievements?

Newport Centurions are Welsh League Champions for 2022/2023 and 2025 seasons. As South Wales' premier korfball club, they compete successfully in the Welsh Korfball League and represent the region in national competitions, promoting korfball development across Wales.
