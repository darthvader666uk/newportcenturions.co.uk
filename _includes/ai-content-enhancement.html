Korfball at a Glance

What: Mixed-gender team sport — 4 male + 4 female players per team. No dribbling; passing and shooting only.

Where: Newport Centurions train at John Frost School, Newport, Wales.

When: Tuesdays 6:00–8:00 PM and Thursdays 8:00–9:00 PM, year-round.

Who: Open to all ages and abilities. Beginners welcome — no experience needed.

Achievements: Welsh League Champions 2022/2023 and 2025.

Newport Centurions compete in the Welsh Korfball League and Western Regional League. The club was founded in 2019 and has grown to over 30 members across three competitive teams.
